Research and development of sustainable desalination technology

About

Vision

To be an international reference for innovation in the water desalination sector, providing this resource internationally in a sustainable manner.

Mision

Create a simple desalination system, without brine as a residue, based on solar energy, implemented in the sea, easy to manufacture and low cost.

Cause

We emerged as a solution to the major problems in seawater desalination:

  • Brine as waste

  • High energy cost

  • Expensive infrastructure

  • Fixed and onshore location

Values

  • Transparency: Providing information to customers.

  • Sustainability: Solar energy and no waste.

  • Innovation: Continuous improvement.

  • Simplicity: The best solution is always simple.

  • Availability: Materials on the market.
